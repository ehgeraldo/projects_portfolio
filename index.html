Projetos em Ciência de Dados

Credit Card Fraud Detection

Blocker Fraud Company is a company specialized in the detection of fraud in financial transactions made through mobile devices.

The company is expanding in Brazil and, to find new customers more quickly, it has adopted a very aggressive strategy.


The strategy works as follows:

  • The company will receive 25% of each transaction value that was correctly detected as fraud;
  • The company will receive 5% of each transaction value that was detected as a fraud despite being legitimate;;
  • The company will return 100% of each transaction value that was detected as legitimate despite being a fraud.

    • The final solution includes a Power BI reporting dashboard.

    Tools

    • Python, Pandas, Numpy, Matplotlib,Seaborn, Plotly, Sklearn
    • Git, Github, Virtual Environment, Linux.
    • Spark, Koalas
    • Machine Learning classification

    Sales forecast for pharmacy network

    Rossmann' manages more than 3,000 drugstores in 7 European countries. As a criterion for releasing resources for each unit, there was a selection process to make such resources available for each specific unit:

    Rossmann store managers are tasked with forecasting their daily sales up to six weeks in advance.


    Sales are influenced by numerous factors, including promotions, competition between nearby and distant stores, state holidays, seasonality and location.


    With thousands of individual managers forecasting sales based on their unique circumstances, the accuracy of the results can vary greatly.


    Tools

    • Python, Pandas, Numpy, Seaborn, Sklearn, Boruta.
    • Git, Github, Virtual Environment, Linux.
    • Prediction with Regression through machine Learning
    • Cloud Heroku.

    Identification of properties for purchase and resale

    'House Rocket' is a digital platform with a business model attributed to the purchase and sale of real estate using data as an acquisition criterion.

    The objective is to find the best business opportunities in the real estate market.


    The main strategy is to indicate properties with great locations and low and attractive prices to maximize resale and revenue.

    Identification of properties below the average sale price and definition of the ideal resale price, based on exploratory data analysis in Python.

    Representação de dados de poços de petróleo

    A visualização de dados de poços de petróleo auxilia a tomada de decisão em vários contextos (geologia, petrofísica, direcionamento de poços).

    O objetivo é aplicar diferentes bibliotecas nos dados abertos de diferentes poços.
